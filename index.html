About Us

Goedheid InniSkuur is a warm, rustic, family-run venue set on the border of the Free State, Gauteng, and Mid Vaal.

Surrounded by the beauty of nature, you & your guests can exhale any stress and inhale the sky above, earth below, and peace within.

"If you truly love nature you will find beauty everywhere." – Vincent van Gogh

We host special functions from birthdays, kitchen teas, bachelorettes, baby showers, weddings (elopement, micro & traditional), to family gatherings.

We can comfortably accommodate up to 100 guests.
